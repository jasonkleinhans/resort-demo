Swiss-Namibian Hospitality

Escape to Serenity

Are you seeking a peaceful retreat enveloped in natural beauty? Whether you are planning a memorable family celebration overlooking the panoramic lake and mountains, or concluding an adventurous Namibian safari and looking for the perfect oasis to unwind and reflect on your journey—you have found your destination.

Guided by our philosophy, "Body and Soul in Harmony with Nature," our mission at Lake Oanob Resort is to preserve the untouched beauty of our landscape. Nestled among rolling hills and rugged mountains, the tranquil waters and our dedicated team create a true sanctuary for your wellbeing. We are committed to offering you unparalleled peace, beauty, and recreation throughout your stay.

Spanning 6,200 hectares in the beautiful Hardap Region—and located just 100 kilometers south of Windhoek—Lake Oanob Resort features a premium selection of spacious luxury chalets, cozy rooms, and scenic waterfront campsites. With breathtaking lake views and genuine, heartfelt hospitality, it is the ultimate destination to relax, reset, and feel invigorated.

Elevate your stay with our year-round activities, including guided game drives, scenic hiking, boat cruises, canoeing, aqua-cycling, and refreshing outdoor pools, or enjoy a sunset cocktail at our signature Giraffe Pool Bar.
