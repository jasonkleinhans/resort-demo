The Story of Lake Oanob

Built around the majestic Oanob Dam just outside Rehoboth, Lake Oanob Resort is an extraordinary contrast of crisp, blue waterfront and rugged African bushveld savanna.

Spanning over 6,200 hectares of majestic nature reserve, we offer peace, tranquility, and exhilarating water experiences designed for international tourists looking for standard Namibian luxury and local residents looking for a pristine weekend getaway.
